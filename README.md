## Task 1: AI Website Copy Generator for Local Businesses

## 1. Business Profile  

•	Business Name: Atthi Hospital  

•	Location: Gudiyattam, Tamil Nadu 

•	Goal: To build trust within the local community by highlighting advanced medical technology combined with a compassionate, professional tone.  

•	Target Audience: Local residents of Gudiyattam seeking reliable, specialized healthcare without traveling to major cities.

## 2. The Structured Prompt (The "Work")

To generate this content, I designed a Master Prompt using persona-setting, clear delimiters, and tone constraints to avoid "robotic" AI outputs.
The Prompt:

> Imagine yourself as a healthcare copywriter for Atthi hospital located at gudiyattam.
>
>  1. You have to create homepage content as your work for the hospital that includes:  
>      	• headline:  hospital name and catchy headline, keep it short and crisp  
>      	• sub headline: include the mantra that is suitable for the hospital  
>      	• about us section: provide details about the hospital  
>         a)	specialized fields (like pediatrics, orthopedic, physiotherapy, surgery, emergency medicine, psychology).  
>         b)	about the hospital and it's advanced technology.  
>
>  2. Create service page: mention every service provided by the hospital with a short description and the respective doctors name  
>
>  3. CTA(call to action) section: include options like booking appointment with categories   as :  
>         a)	Urgent(for emergencies) ,  
>         b)	Friendly(for children),  
>         c)	Professional(consultation)  
> 
>Keep the tone professional and reassuring, avoid sounding AI. highlight important keywords.
